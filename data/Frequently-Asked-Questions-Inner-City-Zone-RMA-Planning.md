Otherwise the<br>parking requirement depends on the kind of activity as detailed in Appendix<br>10 (along with loading, manoeuvring, surfacing and other standards). |**Can I live in the Inner City zone?**<br>Yes as long as it is not on the ground floor of buildings on the main streets of<br>the CBD. Outdoor living courts are to be provided: at least 25m2 for up to 2<br>bedrooms and at least 40m2 for three or more bedrooms. Units not on the<br>ground floor are to have a balcony of at least 12m2.|ICr.40|
|**What do I need to know about buildings on the main streets of the**<br>**CBD?**<br>Additional provisions relate to buildings that front the central portions of<br>Trafalgar, Hardy and Bridge Streets. These include minimum façade height,<br>façade external design and appearance, verandahs and display windows.<br>Buildings must also comply with daylight admission standards as measured<br>from the streets and parking squares.|Various|
